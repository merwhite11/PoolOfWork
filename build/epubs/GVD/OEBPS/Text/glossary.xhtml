Glossary of Terms:

Every vineyard has many blocks

Every block has many rows

Every row has many trunks

Every trunk has 2 arms (bilaterals),

but sometimes 4 (quads), and sometimes 1(unilaterals)

Every arm has at least 5 nodes, but no more than 8

Some nodes push out 1 shoot, some nodes push out 2 (quatas)

Every shoot grows many laterals, leaves and tendrils

Every shoot grows 1, 2, 3 or 4 clusters (racimos)

Every cluster is made up of many, many grapes (uvas)
